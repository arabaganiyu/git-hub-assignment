Fluffy Panckaes

Fluffy Panckakesare light, airy, and perfect for breakfast or brunch. serve them with syrup, butter, or fresh fruits for a delicious start to your day.

Recipe Information

Preparation Time:5 minutes

Cooking Time:15 minutes

Number of Servings:7 Panckakes

Difficulty Level:Beginner

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all- purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 3 teaspoons baking powder
  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 3 tablespoons melted butter

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  2. In another bowl, mix milk, egg, melted butter, and vanilla extract until combined.
  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined
  4. Heat a lightly greased pan over medium heat.
  5. Pour 1/4 cup of batter for each panckake. Cook until bubbles form on the surface, then flip and cook until golden brown.
  6. Serve warm with maple syrup, honey, or fresh fruits.

Tips

For extra fluffiness, let the batter rest for 5 minutes before cooking.
